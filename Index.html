Ohm's Law
Quantity Symbol Unit of Measurement Meaning Formula
Voltage V/E Volts Force that makes electrons move. V/E = I X R
Current I Amperes Flow of electrons per second I = V/E / R
Resistance R Ohms force opposing the flow of electrons R = V/E / I
Power P Watts Electrical Energy transferred by the circuit per second P = V/E *I
